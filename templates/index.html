Gesture Control

Upload PNG Files

Instructions:

  • Upload your PNG presentation slides.
  • Use the "Index Finger Raised" gesture to move to the next slide.
  • Use the "Thumb Raised" gesture to move to the previous slide.
  • Use the "Index and Middle Finger Raised" gesture to draw on the current slide.
  • Use the "Index, Middle, and Ring Finger Raised" gesture to erase the last drawn annotation.
